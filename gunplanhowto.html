Welcome to Gunplan

Just unboxed your latest Gundam masterpiece? Let’s dive into the build, simplified. Gunplan is your co-pilot from start to finish.

Launch the App & Scan

Open Gunplan and point your camera at the kit’s box or select it from your digital collection. Your model is identified in seconds.

Find Parts with AR

Activate the AR Part Finder and hover your device over the runners. Parts illuminate for effortless identification.

Step-by-Step 3D Builds

Follow interactive 3D instructions. Visualize each connection and use voice commands for a hands-free experience.

Share Your Build

Finished building? Showcase your model on your Gunplan profile and connect with a global community of builders.
